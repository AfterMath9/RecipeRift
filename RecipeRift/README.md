# RecipeRift

## What is the app?

RecipeRift is a recipe discovery app that helps you find meals based on the ingredients you already have at home. You can manage your kitchen, explore thousands of recipes, save the ones you like, and keep track of what you have cooked.

## Who is it for?

It is for people who cook at home and want to make better use of what they already have in their fridge and pantry instead of always buying new things.

## What problem does it solve?

The problem is that most recipe apps just show you random recipes without caring about what you actually have at home. RecipeRift checks your kitchen first and then suggests recipes that match your ingredients. This helps reduce food waste and makes cooking easier.

## Main Features

- **My Kitchen** — You can add your ingredients with the category, quantity, and expiry date. You can also edit or delete them. If you pick some ingredients, you can search for recipes that use them.
- **Smart Suggestions** — The home screen shows recipe suggestions based on what you have in your kitchen. Each suggestion shows how many of your ingredients it needs.
- **Explore** — You can browse recipes by category like Beef or Seafood, or by cuisine like Italian or Japanese. There is also a section that shows the newest recipes added to the database.
- **Recipe Detail** — Shows the full recipe with all ingredients, measurements, and cooking steps. The ingredients you already have are marked with a green checkmark. There is also a link to a YouTube video if available.
- **My Collection** — All the recipes you saved are here. You can mark them as cooked, give them a rating, write notes, and take a photo of how your dish turned out.
- **Recipe Search** — You can search by name or let the app search using multiple ingredients at once.
