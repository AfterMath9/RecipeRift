About RecipeRift

RecipeRift eliminates the everyday struggle of answering "what's for dinner?". By letting you input the ingredients sitting in your fridge, we open up a world of culinary possibilities without requiring a trip to the store. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner trying to use up leftover vegetables, RecipeRift adapts to your kitchen journey.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamentally important to us. RecipeRift minimizes data collection, ensuring that your culinary secrets and personal details remain yours alone. All recipe data and custom preferences are stored securely. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

If you choose to synchronize data across devices, your data is securely transmitted and encrypted using industry standard protocols.
